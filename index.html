Who We Are

An Atelier
Built to Execute.

Innerloop Atelier is Dhaka's only studio where brand thinking, spatial design, and landscape architecture operate under one roof — led by a core team of architects, graphic designers, and nurserymen.

We are not a consultancy. We don't hand over decks and disappear. Every project we take on ends with something tangible — built, printed, planted, or launched. That is the Innerloop standard.

"Identity and space are not decorations. They are the infrastructure of how a business is perceived."

— Studio Philosophy · Innerloop Atelier
