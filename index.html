About Us

A studio of specialists,
not generalists.

Innerloop Atelier is a Dhaka-based design studio operating at the intersection of architecture, brand, and landscape. We staff every project through to delivery — on-site, in-print, and hands-in-soil.

We do not dabble. Each service line is led by specialists who go deep: architects, trained designers, qualified nurserymen. The result is a single studio that can take a brief from strategy to physical reality.
